Matt Hackney
Founder

Over 20 years ago, HardMagic opened as a web design firm ran only by Matt in his home-office.  Matt has coached people internationally on his design practices, brand aesthetics, and cultural outreach.  HardMagic now has a family of designers spread across the world who have advised or built some of the leading lifestyle brands in today’s market.

Matt spends his time in the forest for inspiration and continues to choreograph design for Silicon Valley, small-town America, and startups around the world.  He enjoys finding new apprentices who are passionate to learn about design and spends much of his time molding them into future Creative Directors.
